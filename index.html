The Instrutions
  • Once start is pressed a sequence of squares will light up with the colors of blue, red or green.
  • Your job is to remember both the position and the color of this sequence.
  • Once the sequence ends it is your turn. Select the correct colours, using the paintbrushes and press the corresponding squares in the correct order.
  • If you get the order right, congratulations! Try a higher difficulty and test your upper limits.
  • If not, no sweat. Practice and you'll get better.
